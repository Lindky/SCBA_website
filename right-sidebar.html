MEMBERSHIPS

Memberships are open to all bioscientists, including principal investigators, staff, postdoctoral fellows, and graduate students, with either Chinese or none-Chinese heritage.

Life-time members or regular members: principle investigatorand equivalent.

Student members: graduate students, postdoctoral fellows (one year renewable membership).

Membership fee:

Life Regular(residing in NA): $500 one time dues

Regular(residing in NA): $50/Year

Life International(outside NA): $400 one time dues

International(outside NA): $40/Year

Trainees: $15/Year
